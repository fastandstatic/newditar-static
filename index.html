Contact us

Our team is ready to contact you

1. What is your Minimun order quantity?

We have MOQ for international shipments of 1 container, which can be 20 ft or 40 Hq. The container can be completed with different types of products, bags without printing, bags with printing, pos rolls, greaseproof paper, etc.

2. What is your Minimun printing quantity?

To customize a paper bag with your design, the amount you require must be at least 25,000 bags. If you meet the minimum printing quantity, but you cannot fill one container, you can also write to us, we will share your requirement with one of our distributors in the USA or Canada.

3. Can I ask for mockups and samples?

 Yes, let us know in your request that you want to see samples or mockups, we will send it to you as quickly as possible and with no additional cost.

 
4. How many colors can you print in the bag?

 Our machines can print up to 4 colors.

 
5. What are the weights of the papers you use?

 Our papers are from 80 to 120 gsm, which guarantee high quality and resistance.

 
6. What other products can I find in your portfolio?

We are specialists in Shopping and Delivery paper bags with twist handle, However, you can also ask us for bakery bags, party bags, paper bags with hot stamping high-end bags, take out boxes, greaseproof wrapping paper and register paper rolls. Please note that we do NOT make SOS bags.

7. Is your company originally from the USA or Colombia? 

We are a factory of paper bags originally from Colombia, but most of our operations consist of shipping paper bags to North America, in fact we send on average 60 containers per month to the USA.

8. If we purchase from your company, how about the delivery?

Once you complete the container, you can choose between the FOB: We leave the cargo in the port of Barranquilla, Colombia, and the logistics from there are taken care of by you, or CIF: We take the cargo to the port of your choice in your country, and from there the logistics are taken care of by you.  If you require another type of incoterm, you can ask us.

9. If I want to place a large order for the whole year, can you store my products and partially ship them?

 Yes, we have floor agreement, that is, we can produce all your order of the year, we will store it in our finished product warehouse, and we will send the number of containers you require each time you indicate.
